Customize your keyboard.

Maximize your Gaming Experience.

Keycaps

Key caps bring you mostly the aesthetic looks on your keyboard, whether RGB or not. In choosing the keycaps, it depends on what feel and designs you prefer.

Linear Switches

Gamer's prefer switches due to its light pressing force which is good for fast paced games and to avoid finger fatigue

Tactile Switches

Mixed hybrid of linear and tactile. Has a slight bump noise when pressed. It can be used for either typing and gaming.

Clicky Switches

Most noisiest of the three. Can be compared to typing in a type writer. Prefered keyboard in typing due to the satisfaction of the typing sound.

Lube

Lube's purpose is to reduce the scratchiness of the keyswitches when pressed down and for keyboards enthusiast, applying lube brings the satisfying sound of pressing the switches.

Lube Brush

For applying the lube in the keyswitches. The thinner the better for more control and to target specific areas inside the keyswitch.

Keyswitch Opener

Opening a switch is a troublesome when using only fingers. Using an opener would be efficient and safe.

Keyswitch Puller

Use to remove the keyswitch from the keyboards. But remember before using, have a research on what type of keyboard you have. You might have the soldered switch which cannot be removed by a puller.
